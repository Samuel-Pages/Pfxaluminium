Who Are We?

ProFabX Aluminium is a dynamic and reliable Aluminium fabrication company committed to delivering high-quality finishing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. With a strong focus on craftsmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we provide durable and aesthetically pleasing Aluminium installations tailored to meet modern building standards.

Currently based in Anambra State, we serve clients across Nigeria with professionalism, timely delivery, and attention to detail that sets us apart in the fabrication industry.

We are proud to be officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, affirming our commitment to professionalism, transparency, and trust. Our certificate number: 9368455.
